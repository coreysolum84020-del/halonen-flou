Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

HALONEN FLOU is committed to delivering high-quality music services. Please read this policy carefully before purchasing.

Music Lessons ($150 / $250)

Cancellation: You may cancel or reschedule a session up to 24 hours before the scheduled time for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours are non-refundable.

Refunds: If the session was not delivered due to our fault, you will receive a full refund within 5–7 business days.

Artist Promotion ($400)

Cancellation: You may cancel within 48 hours of payment for a full refund, provided the campaign has not yet launched. Once campaign work has begun, no refund is available.

Delivery: Promotion campaigns begin within 3 business days of payment confirmation.

Artist Production ($650)

Cancellation: You may cancel within 48 hours of payment for a full refund, provided production work has not begun. Once studio work starts, no refund is available.

Delivery: Production sessions are scheduled within 5 business days of payment confirmation.

How to Request a Refund

Email us at bluealikeu@gmail.com or call +1 (929) 503-9212 with your name, payment date, and reason for the request. We aim to respond within 1 business day.

Approved refunds are processed through QuickBooks Payments and typically appear in your account within 5–7 business days.

Disputes

If you believe you have been charged incorrectly, please contact us before filing a dispute with your bank. We resolve billing issues quickly and fairly.
